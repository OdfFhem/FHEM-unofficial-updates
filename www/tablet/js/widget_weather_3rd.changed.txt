*** 2019-12-07 ***
  - adjust warn display

    new attribute data-warn-class
    - with usage of data-warn-class size of warn-value
      is independent from size of image
    new attribute data-warn-icon
    - instead of a value you can display a special icon

    example: data-warn-class="bigger"
             data-warn-icon="fa-exclamation"
